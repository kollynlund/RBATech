Royal Business Academy (RBA) is a 12 week residential program in the Czech Republic where participants can learn proven principles of effective business management. The program is designed for individuals with an entrepreneurial mindset. Participants will:

  • Study and learn to apply key business management tools and principles that have significance in today's rapidly changing environment.
  • Develop skills in critical thinking and problem solving.
  • Strengthen their presentation, communication, and marketing skills.
  • Develop leadership skills needed to manage their own company.
  • Gain an appreciation of business ethics and the importance of personal integrity.
  • Establish a personal network by developing lasting relationships with program peers and mentors, and by learning new social media skills.
  • Acquire entrepreneurial skills while developing and executing a viable business plan for a new or existing business.

Attend to learn new business skills from experienced and successful faculty members and use the opportunity to start or grow your own business. Learn skills that will help you develop and strengthen your role as a leader in your family, community, and profession.

About the Cost: The real cost for us to operate this program, including instruction, meals, accommodations, and all other related expenses, is more than EUR 9,000 per student. In future years, we may ask students to pay a larger portion of this cost to attend. However, this year, participants are asked only to pay EUR 1,000 and any who can't afford to pay can receive a scholarship. Why do we do this? Because we want to offer this program to EVERY young man and young woman of character and professional ability who wishes to become an outstanding leader in their community. How can we afford to do this? We know our program will help you be successful and we believe in you. We believe that if you follow the principles we teach, your business will be successful. In exchange for your ability to attend this program at a reduced price, we invite you to offer us a small amount of ownership in the business you create during the program. This way, our success is dependent on helping you be as successful as possible.
